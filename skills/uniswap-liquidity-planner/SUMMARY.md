# uniswap-liquidity-planner
Plan and generate deep links for creating liquidity positions on Uniswap V2, V3, and V4 with concentrated liquidity range support.

## Highlights
- Plan liquidity positions across Uniswap V2, V3, and V4
- Generate Uniswap deep links for LP position creation
- Concentrated liquidity range configuration for V3 and V4 positions
- Fee tier selection and pool discovery workflows
- Part of the uniswap-driver plugin alongside the swap planner
- Multi-chain support across all 12 Uniswap-supported networks
- AI-guided LP planning with range and fee tier recommendations
- Pairs with uniswap-swap-integration for full DeFi composability
